Warm Up Your Holiday Season with Kilishi

Popular Nigerian Snack

Kilishi is a popular Nigerian snack made from dried and seasoned meat, typically beef or goat meat. The meat is thinly sliced, dried in the sun or using a machine, and then seasoned with a blend of spices,herbs, and sometimes peanut sauce. Kilishi is often served as a snack or appetizer and the is known for its chewy texture, savory flavor, and aromatic smell.
